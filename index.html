About


Planet Of The Crepesbegan in Thailand, where my wife and I were backpacking for 2 months, eating crepes and drinking smoothies like a couple of maniacs. That’s when we decided to bring this fresh, nutritious, and delicious concept back home for everyone else to enjoy.

At Planet Of The Crepes we strive to give you the best experience possible. Our food is made right in front of you, ensuring you’re always getting fresh and quality food you can trust.

We also believe its important to give back, so we are proud to announce that a portion of all profits will be donated to help impoverished children live a happy life in Thailand. Gift of Happiness Foundation offers interactive comedy shows, along with essential medical and educational supplies that goes to help as many people as possible.

Mihran Sirekan
